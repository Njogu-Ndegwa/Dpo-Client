Instructions on how to connect your domain.

  1. Log in to your domain host, go to the domain management area and find the DNS editing options.Create a new CNAME record with the following settings:
    • Host/​Alias: www
    • Destination/​Target: s.eu-multiscreensite.com
  2. Point the root of your domain (domain.com) to your website. To do this, create two A records with the following settings:
    • First
    • Host/​Alias: @
    • Destination/​Target: 18.197.248.23
    • Second
    • Host/​Alias: @
    • 52.5.120.70
